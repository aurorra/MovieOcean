About MovieOcean

MovieOcean is a movie recommender system. For the recommendations, we are using a user-based collaborative filtering algorithm. We build neighborhoods according to the personalities of the users. For assessing the personalities, we are using the Five-Factor Model: Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism. For short: OCEAN. The findings of this system are of academic interest.

Privacy Policy

In order to use our system, the user is required to sign up with an email address and a password. We additionally ask for the age, gender, and country of the user. This information is only used for computing recommendations or evaluating our system. No data is released to any third party. Our conclusions might include certain personality types, but there is no possibility to trace back results to a certain email address. We might use your email address to send reminders or similar with the purpose of making our system more useful.

Credits

For our system, we are using the movie database TMDB. In addition, the questions for the personality questionnaire were taken from the International Personality Item Pool (IPIP). The information about the big five personality traits were mostly taken from: Roccas, Sonia, et al. "The big five personality factors and personal values." Personality and social psychology bulletin 28.6 (2002): 789-801. Available at https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/abs/10.1177/0146167202289008 .
