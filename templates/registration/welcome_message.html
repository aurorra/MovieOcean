Welcome!

Before we can give you movie recommendations based on your personality, we need you to fill out a questionnaire so that the system gets to know you. All of your information is safe with us. We, the developers, do not look into your personality. Your ratings are analyzed after some time but we do not connect the ratings to you as a person. Your age, gender and country are also merely used to better estimate the prediction power of our system.

In order for this Recommender System to work, we need you to rate some movies. Therefore we would really appreciate it if you could rate at least ten movies that you enjoyed watching in the past. Don't worry, there will be a reminder after the questionnaire. When you complete the questionnaire, you can always get your movie recommendations by clicking on Recommendations in the menu section Movies above. We will remind you 7 days after your registration to rate the recommendations. If you do not want to receive this reminder, feel free to opt out. You can do so by clicking on Account in the navigation above. There, you will see a button that lets you deactivate the rating reminders.
