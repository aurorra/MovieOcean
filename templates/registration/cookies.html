Cookies

We only use two types of strictly necessary cookies: the sessionid, which is needed for you to sign in and use our personality-based Recommender System and the csrf token, which is used to prevent CSRF attacks on your account. CSRF attacks can be used to carry out actions with your account unintentionally. If you want to know more about the topic, have a look at this site.

We also store other information about you but not in the form of cookies. Particularly, we store your age, your country, your gender and your email in our database, once you register. Also, if you use our system, we store your Big Five personality scores and your movie ratings. This information is only stored to improve the quality of the movie recommendations and to asses the prediction power of the personality-based recommendation approach. Your information will not be shared with any third parties.
